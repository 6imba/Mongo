Q. If Nosql is schema less why do we have schema in mongoose?
	- When people say that MongoDB “has no schema”, they really mean that it does not enforce schema the way SQL databases do.
	- MongoDB pushes schema concerns up to your application level, where you can handle them more flexibly.
	- https://stackoverflow.com/questions/13107976/why-does-mongoose-use-schema-when-mongodbs-benefit-is-supposed-to-be-that-its
	- https://www.mongodb.com/unstructured-data/schemaless

Q. Questions to Answer Before Choosing a Database?
	- What type of data will you be analyzing?
	- How much data are you dealing with?
	- What kind of resources can you devote to the setup and maintenance of your database?
	- Do you need real-time data?
